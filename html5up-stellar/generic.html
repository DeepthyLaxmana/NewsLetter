Exec Corner

As many of you have hopefully heard, the AMS Custom Reporting team is now part of the Client Success organization. But what does that mean? And how will it work having a team that is aligned to AMS clients not being in the AMS organization?

These are a couple of questions you may have had - which I know in KC have been a lesser question than "which campus will we be at". Our directive from senior leadership is an exciting challenge for all of us. How can we make reporting and analytics better for our clients and across Cerner?

Over the last several years, the MAS Custom Reporting team has been improving operations, tooling, and client facing training. It has been an increasingly high client satisfaction service team that solves client problems(something I am proud of this group for!). With the adition of the Analytics Consulting and Value Advisory Analytics teams, we are challenged to not only continue to make AMS' service better, but to leverage best practices and innovations from all teams to improve each service. Day-to-day I do not expect our work to change greatly, and we will obviously be tightly tied to all other parts of the AMS organization, but we will be working hard to solve universal challenges clients face and while discovering new opportunities to continue to grow the business.

- Adam
