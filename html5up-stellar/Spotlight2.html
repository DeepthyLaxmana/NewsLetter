Associate Spotlight - Stephen Sedgwick

When i get discouraged on my day like many of us do, I think to all the things that i have done. The places and people I have met and been able to help along the way. This is what drives my day to day interactions. Spending time with ens users on the phone and in person. Visiting hospitals to understand the reason why we are needed and why what we do helps. When i approach a situation, I try to remind myself, I have been here before. This is from figuring out an issue to helping someone through an existing problem. I do contribute to a statistic however; I make up a vital role like everyone else and don't believe in being a statistic.

What keeps me motivated?
My biggest motivation is People. Helping others and empowering people for that extra drive to get going. Encouraging and collabourating with colleagues to adapt to change, removing obstructions for the team, building and supporting our software to empower our clients for better care... everything excites me. I love my work as it brings the feeling of fulfilment and joy, that i can contribute my bit to the society by being part of healthcare industry.
