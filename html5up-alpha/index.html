Success Stories

“Happiness can be found in the darkest of times, only if one remembers to switch on the light.” And I guess this quote surely summarizes my philosophy of life.
Having grown up in Kanpur, I was an extremely introverted person (I have become more of an ambivert now). When I enrolled in VIT, Vellore for my bachelor’s is when my perception towards life changed. That’s when I came out of the cocoon.
Before Cerner, I interned for an Oil and Gas Business Analysis company, and Cerner came to me at a time when frankly, I didn’t have much to look forward to. I joined Cerner in 2015 as a Delivery Consultant for AMS. We were the first batch of consultants hired for AMS to work US 2-11 full time. I started working with Pathnet and soon my interest peeked understanding the kind of work we do and what impact it has on society. This motivated me to give in my all to the work I did, and Cerner never failed to notice and appreciate me for my dedication and efforts. In a short span of close to 3 years, I started managing a team of engineers in AMS/SWx. Being a lead wasn’t easy at first. But seeing the zeal and dedication in the eyes of the associates I work with daily, I realized I need to buckle up to be the best version of myself, not just for my associates but even for my own self. My mantra that I always tell and share with my associates is the same that I applied for me when I worked on issues. The issues we resolve for the clients, impact people directly. So, we are the background heroes that enable healthcare givers to do their job effectively.
During my college, I was a part of an NGO called – Make a Difference (MAD), which deals with underprivileged kids. Once I came to Bangalore and my life stabilized a bit, I joined MAD again and volunteered there as an English teacher as well as a Mentor for new teachers teaching them the tips and tricks on how to deal with kids. MAD taught me empathy and patience which I apply day to day in my life here at Cerner as well. Along with this, in my free time, I am a huge movie buff. I write reviews on Instagram and manage a page called “Chai and Reviews”. Do go and read it if you get time.
