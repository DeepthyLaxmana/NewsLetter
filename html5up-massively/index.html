Ben Hilmes Joins ITWorks Family

John Peterzalek, Executive Vice President and Chief Client and Services Officer, officially announced on 7th July 2020 about the transformation journey that resulted in evolving and adapting organization structure to enhance the client experience and advance strategies that keep our clients and Cerner ahead of the competition. To that end, he explained the realignment of our relationship segments to provide more focused support across each of our defined client segments. ITWorks BU will be included under Premier and will be led by Ben HIlmes, Snr VP with Dick Flanigan, Snr VP, transition out of ITWorks to lead our regulatory alignment, federal policy efforts, segment market strategies and managing relationships with significant industry partners.

Ben expressed his excitement on this email to join the ITWorks Community.

We wish him the best and look forward to his leadership and guidance to advance the Cerner ITWorks 2.0 strategy.
