Pam's message

It is my pleasure to present the Soarian AMS, CAS and Client Support Q1 & Q2 2020 Newsletter. The publication of the newsletter was put on hold at the end of teh first quarter as we were in the very begining stages of the COVID-19 pandemic. Little did we know what we were all in store for... we were attempting to navigate everyone working at home, trying to find balance between work/home life and some sort of normalcy, enduring the fear and uncertainty of potentially contracting the virus, and working diligently to helo our clients stay ahead of the pending insurmountable impacts of this awful virus. The term 'Unprecedented Times' doesn't seem quite accurate enough. Please take a few minutes and review all of the great information provided herein. This publication is dedicated to each and every one of who has done a tremendous job over the past 2 quaters adjusting to our 'new normal' and navigating any/all challenges you faced, be they personal or professional. enjoy the read!

Thank you for everything you do for Cerner and our clients!

Best,
Pam
